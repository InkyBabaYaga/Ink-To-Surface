Ancestral Echoes

Created: 08/21/23

In the river of my soul, stories reside,
Ancestral whispers, a timeless guide,
Blood's connection through ages past,
Bridging the present, forever to last.

With every beat, a saga unfolds,
Legends of yore in our veins, they're told,
Tales of courage, of love and despair,
Blood's ancient echoes, a heritage rare.

Through the stream of time, we find our kin,
Linked by blood, a bond that's within,
History's chapters, etched in our code,
In the river of life, their wisdom bestowed.

A symphony of lives, harmoniously spun,
As blood's rich narrative, forever is spun,
Stories woven together, a tapestry grand,
In the river of my soul, they shall withstand.
