INK TO SURFACE

By Ersell Kizzie

Hello, my name is Ersell Kizzie. I am your author.

This poem collection started in the physical world—notebooks filled with ink and raw emotion. I spent years refining these pieces, moving them from paper to digital surfaces, taking breaks when the weight was too much and returning when the words were ready.

This is my archive. This is where the ink meets the surface.
