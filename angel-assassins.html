Angel Assassins

Created: March 24, 2023

They arrive in a crackle of white light- a force divine weight
Then, silence follows, heavy and peaceful, like a dream you can’t wake from.

Their faces are stone, but if you look closely at their eyes,
There is a flicker of disappointment.
They wear the skin of your distant cousins, hiding in plain sight until the mission begins.

They are masters of the edge: hand-to-hand, sword, dagger.
Do not be fooled by the gentle way they move; they own the dark of night.

An elite, silent army finishing a victory before the battle starts. A divine legacy that burns forever.
